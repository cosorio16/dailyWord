WELCOME

This game consists of guessing a 5-letter word. There is no time limit, and you can switch between light and dark modes using the Your progress is saved, so you can return anytime. You have 6 attempts to guess the word.

Enter a word, press enter, and the letters will take the following colors:

  • B

    The letter exists in the word and is in the correct position.

  • Y

    The letter exists but is not in the correct position.

  • R

    The letter does not exist in the word.
